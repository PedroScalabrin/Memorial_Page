Who is Elon Musk?

Elon Musk is known as one of the most daring entrepreneurs of recent times, as he made highly technological and innovative projects possible. Among the companies he founded or commanded are names like Paypal, Tesla, SpaceX and Neuralink.

His career also draws attention due to the diversity of his areas of expertise; geolocation, clean energy, mobility, space exploration, artificial intelligence and neuroscience.

Achivements

  • CEO and CTO of SpaceX
  • CEO and founder of Neutralink
  • CO-chairman of OpenAI
  • CO-founder of Tesla
  • CO-founder of SolarCity
  • CO-founder of Zip2
